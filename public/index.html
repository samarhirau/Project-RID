What is RID Organization?

RID Organization i.e. Research, Innovation and Discovery Organization is an organization which is run by TWF(TWKSAA WELFARE FOUNDATION). The main objective of which is that in the coming times, the discovery, publication and use of NEW (RID, PMS & TLR) should be done first in Indian culture, civilization and language from this holy land of India.
